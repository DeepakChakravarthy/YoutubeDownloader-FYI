PROCEDURE TO DOWNLOAD VIDEO


    Download Procedure For Youtube Manager.

  • First, go to the YouTube page containing the video you want to download.

  • When you've found the page, press Ctrl+L on your keyboard and then Ctrl+C to copy. or Right Click on video Select Copy URL Option.

  • Enter the URL of a YouTube video Paste inside the text box by pressing Ctrl+V in YouTube Tab.

  • After Select Valid Option, and then click Download button.

  • Select Download Location to Save the file.


    Download Procedure For Facebook Manager.

  • First, go to the Facebook timeline containing the video you want to download.

  • When you've found the Video, Right Click on video Select Copy URL Option(On old facebook Timeline). or Click Option that shown in Post Right side Top Corner(3 Dot) Select " Embed " Copy URL Shown in Embed page.

  • Enter the URL of a Facebook video Paste inside the text box by pressing Ctrl+V in Facebook Tab.

  • After Select Valid Option, and then click Download button.

  • Select Download Location to Save the file.
